Good Questions

What out-of-state buyers actually ask me.

What's the real cost of living in Brevard County?

Short answer: lower than most coastal Florida markets, higher than inland. The bigger line items to plan for are homeowners insurance, wind/flood coverage if you're near the coast, and HOA/CDD fees in newer neighborhoods. I'll walk you through actual numbers for the areas you're considering.

Do I need a Florida-based agent, or can I use someone from my home state?

You need someone licensed in Florida. More importantly — you need someone who knows the micro-markets here. A great agent in Denver or Nashville can't tell you why a house on A1A and a house two blocks inland behave differently on insurance.

Is Zillow accurate for Brevard?

For inventory: mostly. For pricing, neighborhood read, school boundaries, and flood/wind zones: not reliably. That's the entire reason this page exists.

What if I'm 6–12 months out?
